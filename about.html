About-Clean Water & Sanitation Awareness Site

Project Title

Clean Water & Sanitation Awareness Site

Brief description

A simple web-based platform providing core information about safe water use, sanitation practices, and hygiene: how to access and store clean water, proper waste disposal, hand washing techniques, and preventing waterborne diseases.

Motivation

The project is motivated by the fact that many communities still lack reliable knowledge on clean water and sanitation. Web technology provides a low-cost way to increase awareness and reduce preventable disease.

Beneficiaries

  • Students who can learn basic hygiene practices
  • Families and households seeking practical guidance
  • Local communities with low sanitation awareness
  • NGOs and health advocates who can share the resource

Potential societal impact

Increased awareness can encourage better hygiene, reduce waterborne illnesses, lower healthcare costs, and support the Sustainable Development Goal of "Clean Water and Sanitation for All."

Share this project

Share the site with community groups, schools, or NGOs. Consider translating content into local languages and adding printable checklists for households.
